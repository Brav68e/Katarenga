BASIC RULES
• The board is initially empty.
• Players take turns placing a pawn on an empty square.
• A pawn cannot be placed where it could be captured by a pawn
  already on the board (regardless of color).
• There are no moves or captures in this game.

OBJECTIVE
The last player able to legally place a pawn wins the game.

STRATEGY
Plan your placements to limit your opponent's options while preserving 
your own possibilities.
