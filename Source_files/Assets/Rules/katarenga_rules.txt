BASIC RULES
• Players take turns moving a pawn.
• Captures are allowed (except on the first turn).

OBJECTIVE
To win:
• Place two pawns in the opponent's camps.
• Or capture enough pawns to block the opponent.

WIN CONDITION
• To enter an opponent's camp: reach the opposite line, then enter it on the next turn.
• A pawn in an opponent's camp is permanent and cannot be moved or captured.
